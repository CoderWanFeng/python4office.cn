🎓 Step 1: Student Certification

First, complete student certification on Alibaba Cloud.

Steps:

  1. Open Alibaba Cloud official website
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Search for student certification
  4. Submit materials (student ID, Xuexin.com screenshots, etc.)
  5. Wait for approval

Notes:

  • Must be a current student
  • Certification has an expiration date, need to re-certify when it expires
  • Student discounts can only be enjoyed after certification is approved

💰 Step 2: Check Discounts

After certification, check the discounts you can enjoy.

Common discounts:

Discount typeDescription

Discount packageStudent-exclusive discount, much cheaper
Free quotaMonthly free quota

🎯 Step 3: Use Discounts Wisely

After getting the discounts, use them wisely.

How to use cost-effectively:

  1. Try the free package first
  2. Buy discount packages only when needed
  3. Pay attention to limited-time promotion activities
  4. Plan usage rationally, don't waste

💡 Final Word

Students have limited budgets, save when you can.

First certify, then check discounts, use wisely, don't waste.
