💰 Case 1: AI Writing, Monthly Income 5k

Background:

Xiao Wang is an office worker who likes writing and wants to do a side hustle.

How he did it:

  1. Used AI Agent to help others write articles and copywriting

Results:

First month: 3k, second month: 5k.

🎨 Case 2: AI Design, Monthly Income 8k

Background:

Xiao Li is a designer with a not-so-busy job who wants to do a side hustle.

How he did it:

  1. Used AI Agent to help others create posters and logos
  2. AI generates the first draft, Xiao Li manually adjusts it
  3. Opens shops on Taobao and Pinduoduo to take orders

Results:

First month: 4k, second month: 8k.

🛠️ Case 3: AI Tool Development, Passive Income

Background:

Xiao Zhang is a programmer who wants to create passive income.

How he did it:

  1. Used AI Agent to develop a small tool
  2. Sells it on platforms
  3. Continuously updates and iterates

Results:

First month: 1k, second month: 2k, third month: 3k - continuous growth.

Passive income is really nice.
