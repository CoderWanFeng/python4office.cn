My heart tightened, thinking I was about to be scolded.

Instead, my boss said:

"Wan Feng did the work of 5 people alone. The efficiency is too high! Everyone should learn from him."

The group exploded with likes and colleagues giving thumbs up.

Someone privately asked me: "Wan Feng, do you work overtime every day?"

Actually, they didn't know—I leave at 6 PM sharp every day, never overtime.

The secret is I used a free AI tool—OpenClaw.

Today, I'm sharing this tool's usage method with you without reservation.

Current Workplace Reality: One Person Doing Three People's Work

Honestly, when I saw the boss's praise, my heart was complicated.

I didn't work harder—I just used the right tools.

Think about today's workplace environment, it's really competitive:

  • Job posting said "one person, one position," but became "one person, multiple positions" after joining
  • Meetings and replying to messages during the day, can only start real work at night
  • Working until 9 PM is normal, weekend on-call all the time
  • 35-year-old crisis looming, worried about being optimized someday

I was like this before, exhausted every day, same salary.

Until I discovered OpenClaw.

My Change: From Overtime to Leaving on Time

Before: Working Until 9 PM Every Day

At this time last year, my life looked like this:

Arrived at 9 AM, first replying to overnight messages—30 unread WeChat, 20 unread DingTalk, 15 unread emails.

After replying to all these, it was already 10:30 AM.

Then various meetings, temporary tasks—a day where I actually had less than 2 hours for real work.

At 7 PM, colleagues started leaving, and I could finally start writing code, making spreadsheets.

Working until 9 PM, so tired I just wanted to go home and sleep.

Weekends? Weekends were for catching up on sleep.

At that time, I thought this was just life.

Now: Leaving at 6 PM Sharp Every Day

In January this year, a friend recommended OpenClaw to me.

Honestly, at first I didn't have high hopes.

But OpenClaw is open source, code on GitHub, anyone can view it.

Free, and transparent.

I spent one evening configuring it, started using it the next day.

After one week, I discovered:

  • No longer manually replying to messages in the morning, AI handles automatically
  • Reports no longer manually made, AI generates automatically
  • Files no longer manually organized, AI classifies automatically

I gained an extra 2-3 hours daily, can leave on time, can work out, can be with family.

My boss thought I was working overtime, but I was just more efficient.

5 Workplace Pain Points I Solved with OpenClaw

Pain Point 1: Too Many Messages, Can't Keep Up with Replies

Me before:

WeChat, DingTalk, email—had to check every platform.

Customer inquiries, colleague questions, boss arrangements—had to reply to every one.

After a day, just replying to messages took 3-4 hours.

Me now:

OpenClaw automatically connects to all platforms, unified message processing.

Common questions get auto-replied (prices, shipping, after-sales, etc.).

Important messages get flagged, trivial matters handled automatically.

Saves 2-3 hours daily.

Pain Point 2: Too Many Reports, Manual Work to the Point of Breakdown

Me before:

Every day exporting data from multiple systems, manually merging Excel sheets.

Weekly generating fixed-format reports, monthly batch sending emails to clients.

Spending 1-2 hours making reports, eyes sore from exhaustion.

Me now:

OpenClaw automatically captures data, automatically merges, calculates, generates reports.

Scheduled tasks, automatically executed at set times.

Saves 1-2 hours daily.

Pain Point 3: Too Many Files, Organization is a Headache

Me before:

Files clients sent scattered across various chat records.

Project document versions in chaos, can't find the latest version.

Spending 2-3 hours weekly organizing files.

Me now:

OpenClaw automatically classifies and saves files, automatically names, archives, backs up.

One-click search when needed.

Saves 2-3 hours weekly.

Pain Point 4: Too Many Meetings, Recording and Organizing is Troublesome

Me before:

3-4 meetings daily, each needs to be recorded.

Organizing meeting minutes after meetings, spending 1-2 hours.

Task assignment and follow-up, lots of time spent.

Me now:

OpenClaw automatically records and transcribes during meetings.

Automatically extracts action items, automatically assigns tasks, sets reminders.

Saves 1 hour per meeting.

Pain Point 5: Too Many Client Responses, Same Questions Answered Repeatedly

Me before:

Same question answered dozens of times—"How much is X?" "How do I install it?" "What are the specs?"

Every time feeling impatient, customers feeling neglected.

Me now:

OpenClaw auto-responds to common questions.

For complex issues, provides suggested responses with one click to send.

Saves 1-2 hours daily, customer satisfaction increased.
