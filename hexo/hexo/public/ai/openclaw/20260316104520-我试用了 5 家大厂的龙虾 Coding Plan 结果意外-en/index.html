I Tried 5 Major Companies' Lobster Coding Plan, Results Were Unexpected

Hello everyone, this is programmer Wan Feng actively working on various AI projects.

In March 2026, a strange phenomenon appeared: Domestic major companies collectively "went off their beaten path," launching AI programming plans targeting the lobster industry.

Tencent, Alibaba, Baidu, ByteDance, Huawei, 5 major companies, 5 different "Lobster Coding Plans". I spent 14 days, tried all of them, and the results were unexpected.

🦞 Why Lobster?

Before starting, I was also confused: Why lobster? Not cat/dog recognition, not face recognition,，偏偏是 lobster?

After in-depth research, I found 3 deep reasons behind this:

1. Large Enough Market

  • 2025 China lobster industry total output: 450 billion yuan
  • Practitioners: Over 10 million
  • AI penetration: Less than 5%, huge growth space

2. Typical Technical Scenarios

Lobster recognition covers multiple core AI scenarios:

  • Image recognition (freshness detection)
  • Classification algorithms (variety, specifications)
  • Time series prediction (price fluctuations)
  • Decision optimization (procurement strategy)

3. Adequate Policy Support

  • Ministry of Agriculture "Digital Village" pilot project
  • Local government subsidies for AI agricultural applications
  • Rural revitalization special fund support
