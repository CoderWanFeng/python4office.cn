The 3 AI Courses I Bought

Promised:

  • Learn AI monetization in 30 days

Actual:

  • Content was all basic introductions, findable online
  • Projects were all theoretical, no hands-on practice
  • After completion, still didn't know where to start

Course 2: AI Programming Master Course (12,800 yuan)

Promised:

  • From zero to mastery
  • Can independently develop AI applications

Actual:

  • Code was ready-made, couldn't modify it
  • Job recommendations were sales push for more expensive courses
  • Q&A responses were slow, basically self-learning

Course 3: AI Self-Media Special Training Camp (7,800 yuan)

Promised:

  • Build account in 7 days
  • Monetize in 30 days
  • Provide material library

Actual:

  • Material library was all downloaded from the internet

Gains: A bunch of unusable materials and full disappointment.
