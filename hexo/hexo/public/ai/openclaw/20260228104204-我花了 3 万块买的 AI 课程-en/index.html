Hello everyone, this is programmer Wan Feng.

At the beginning of 2025, I made a decision.

The salesperson told me: "This is the most comprehensive AI course on the market. After learning, you can use AI to make money."

I believed it and gritted my teeth to pay.

30,000 yuan was not a small amount for me at that time.

After completing it, I realized it wasn't as good as a free open source tool.

Today I'm doing a real comparison to help you save this 30,000 yuan.

The 3 AI Courses I Bought

Course 1: AI Side Hustle Practical Camp (9,800 yuan)

Promised:

  • Learn AI monetization in 30 days
  • Provide 10 money-making projects
  • Earn over 10,000 yuan monthly after completion

Actual:

  • Content was all basic introductions, findable online
  • Projects were all theoretical, no hands-on practice
  • After completion, still didn't know where to start

Course 2: AI Programming Master Course (12,800 yuan)

Promised:

  • From zero to mastery
  • Can independently develop AI applications
  • Job recommendations

Actual:

  • Code was ready-made, couldn't modify it
  • Job recommendations were sales push for more expensive courses
  • Q&A responses were slow, basically self-learning

Course 3: AI Self-Media Special Training Camp (7,800 yuan)

Promised:

  • Build account in 7 days
  • Monetize in 30 days
  • Provide material library

Actual:

  • Material library was all downloaded from the internet
  • Account building methods were outdated
  • Monetization was all luck

Total spent: 30,400 yuan

Gains: A bunch of unusable materials and full disappointment.

Discovering OpenClaw: Free One is Actually Better

When I was disappointed in AI tools, a friend recommended OpenClaw to me.

First feeling: Can a free one actually work?

After one week of trial: True香！

This tool:

  • Open source and free, code on GitHub
  • Powerful features, supports over a dozen platforms
  • Active community, questions answered anytime

Much more practical than the 30,000 yuan course I took.
