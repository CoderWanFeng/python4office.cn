🔥 TOP 2: AI Agent —— The Biggest Trend of 2026

If 2023 was the year of ChatGPT, 2024 was the year of AI application explosion, then 2026 is the Year of the AI Agent.

What is an AI Agent? Simply put:

An AI assistant that can think, plan, and execute autonomously

Unlike regular AI chatbots where you ask and it answers, AI Agents can:

  • ✅ Automatically complete multi-step tasks for you
  • ✅ Plan workflows like a real person
  • ✅ Call various tools to accomplish complex operations

For example: Tell an AI Agent "Help me organize this month's sales data, create a report and send it to the group." It will:

  1. Download data files
  2. Clean and organize
  3. Generate charts
  4. Send emails

One instruction, everything is done!

Now with AI tools, these tasks become super simple:

AI Office Skill is the best solution for these pain points!
