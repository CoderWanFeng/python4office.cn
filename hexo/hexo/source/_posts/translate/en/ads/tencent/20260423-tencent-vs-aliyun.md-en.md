Today a hardcore comparison: **Tencent Cloud Coding Plan (49 yuan/month) vs Alibaba Cloud Bailian (29 yuan/month)**.

Price differs by 20 yuan, which one should you choose?

## 1. Price Comparison

| Product | Price/month | Difference |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| **Tencent Cloud** | 49 yuan | - |
| **Alibaba Cloud** | 29 yuan | 20 yuan cheaper |

Alibaba Cloud is almost half the price, but is the experience really half as good?

## 2. Core Capability Comparison

### 1. Large Models

| Product | Main Model | Features |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Tencent Cloud** | Tencent Hunyuan | First tier domestically |
| **Alibaba Cloud** | Tongyi Qianwen | First tier domestically |

Both are top players among domestic large models, capabilities are comparable.

### 2. Ecosystem Advantages

| Product | Ecosystem | Suitable Scenarios |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| **Tencent Cloud** | WeChat ecosystem | Mini programs, official accounts, WeCom |
| **Alibaba Cloud** | Alibaba ecosystem | Taobao open platform, Alibaba Cloud services |

**Conclusion**: Choose ecosystem based on your project.

### 3. Price and Service

| Product | Price | Free Quota | Enterprise Service |
|---------|-------|------------|-------------------|
| **Tencent Cloud** | 49 yuan/month | Limited | Complete |
| **Alibaba Cloud** | 29 yuan/month | Limited | Complete |

Alibaba Cloud is cheaper, Tencent Cloud service is more for WeChat developers.

## 3. Usage Scenario Comparison

### WeChat Mini Program Development

→ **Choose Tencent Cloud**, WeChat ecosystem seamless integration, more convenient API calls.

### Taobao/E-commerce Related Development

→ **Choose Alibaba Cloud**, better Taobao open platform integration.

### Enterprise Internal Tool Development

→ Both are fine, depends on which cloud your company uses.

### Personal Projects/Learning

→ **Recommend Alibaba Cloud**, 29 yuan/month is cheaper.
