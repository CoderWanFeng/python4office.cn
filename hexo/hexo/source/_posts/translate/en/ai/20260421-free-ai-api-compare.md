## What Is an AI API?

Plain English: **Someone else has trained the AI model, and you can call it through an interface.**

Just like you don't have to build your own power plant; you can just use the national grid's electricity.

**Benefits of using AI APIs:**
- No need to train your own model (saves $1M+)
- No need to buy GPUs (saves $100K+)
- Pay-as-you-go, controllable cost
- Easy integration, clear documentation

- **Strong performance**: DeepSeek-V3 on par with GPT-4o, super-strong coding

**Test data:**
