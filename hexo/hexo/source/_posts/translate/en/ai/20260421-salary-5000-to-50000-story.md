# From a $700/Month Salary to $1,400/Month in Side Income: An Ordinary Person's AI Money-making Journey

> **Reading time:** 8 minutes
> **Audience:** Ordinary people who want to use AI to grow their income
> **Real case:** Submitted by a follower, published with permission

---

"I didn't expect that at 30, I could still learn to use AI from zero."

That was the first sentence Xiaolin (alias) wrote in a message to me.

She is an administrative assistant at a small company, earning ¥5,000 ($700) a month, renting in Wuhan, with no more than ¥1,000 ($140) in savings each month.

"Can't see a future" was the phrase she repeated most during that time.

**Three months later, she told me: "I've been using AI for a side hustle, and now I earn an extra ¥8,000 ($1,120) a month."**

Today, I want to share her story.

---

## 1. The Predicament: ¥5,000/Month, No Future

### 1.1 Xiaolin's Situation

Xiaolin, 30, single, originally from rural Hubei.

She studied administrative management in college and has been doing administrative work since graduating.

"No technical content, can be replaced anytime" — that was how she described her job.

Her monthly salary breakdown:
- Rent: ¥1,500 ($210)
- Living expenses: ¥2,000 ($280)
- Money to family: ¥1,000 ($140)
- Other: ¥500 ($70)
- **Savings: ¥0**

"Sometimes I'm afraid to go to the hospital when I'm sick, in case it costs too much."

---

### 1.2 The Turning Point: A Comment That Changed Her Mind

The turning point came last November.

Xiaolin saw an article online about "How ordinary people can use AI for a side hustle."

"I thought it was another scam, but after reading it, I found that the tools mentioned in the article were all free, and the tutorials were very detailed."

She decided to give it a try.

"**It costs nothing, so trying it won't hurt.**"

---

### 1.3 First Attempt: AI Helps Me Write Articles

Xiaolin's first attempt was writing articles on a public account.

She used ChatGPT to help with writing and combined it with her own work experience. Her first article was *How Administrative Staff Can Use AI to Improve Work Efficiency*.

"I didn't expect this article to go viral, with over 10,000 reads."

That article brought her:
- 300+ new followers on the public account
- 3 ad inquiries (¥500 / $70 in revenue)
- 1 paid consultation (¥200 / $28 in revenue)

**She made her first ¥700 ($98) from AI.**

"I was so excited. Although ¥700 isn't much, it showed me the possibility."

---

## 2. Exploration: Finding the AI Money-making Path That Suits Her

### 2.1 Side Hustles Xiaolin Tried

Over the next two months, Xiaolin tried many AI-related side hustles:

| Side Hustle Type | Result | Monthly Income |
|------------------|--------|----------------|
| Public account writing | Mediocre results | ¥500 ($70) |
| Xiaohongshu posts | Poor data, gave up | ¥0 |
| Xianyu reselling | Needs capital, gave up | ¥0 |
| Zhihu Q&A | Mediocre results | ¥200 ($28) |

"Honestly, that period was depressing. AI wasn't as magical as people said."

---

### 2.2 The Breakthrough: AI Ghostwriting Service

The breakthrough came in January of this year.

In a workplace group, Xiaolin saw someone asking: "Anyone who can help me write a year-end summary? Paid."

She suddenly thought: "**Can I use AI to ghostwrite articles for people?**"

She got to work right away.

She posted a service link on Xianyu: "**AI-assisted writing, ghostwriting all kinds of articles, starting at ¥50 ($7) per article.**"

**Month 1:** 8 orders, ¥1,200 ($168) in revenue
**Month 2:** 15 orders, ¥2,800 ($392) in revenue
**Month 3:** Word of mouth, 23 orders, ¥4,600 ($644) in revenue

---

### 2.3 The Key to Success: What Did Xiaolin Do Right?

I asked her: "Why did your AI ghostwriting work?"

She summarized 3 key lessons:

**✅ 1. Precise positioning, only doing workplace articles**

"I don't take any kind of article. I only do workplace ones: year-end summaries, work reports, job review reports, promotion speeches."

"These articles have high demand, high unit price, and AI is especially good at writing them."

**✅ 2. Quality first, never half-hearted**

"Many AI ghostwriters just generate with AI and hand it to the client. But I don't do that."

"I let AI generate 3-5 versions, then manually select, modify, and polish them to ensure quality."

"Although this is slower, customer satisfaction is high and there are many repeat customers."

**✅ 3. Build reputation, accumulate resources**

"After each order is done, I proactively ask the client if they're satisfied and whether they need any changes."

"Many clients will refer me to their colleagues, so my orders keep growing."

---

## 3. Upgrade: From Side Hustle to Small Business

### 3.1 The Bottleneck

By the 4th month, Xiaolin hit a bottleneck.

"Writing articles after work every day, and on weekends too, but there are too many orders, I can't handle them all."

"And I realized that pure time-for-money has too low a ceiling."

She started thinking: **Can I turn AI ghostwriting into a sustainable business?**

---

### 3.2 Standardized Service Process

Xiaolin did 3 things:

**① Created service packages**
```
Basic (¥50 / $7): Ghostwriting articles under 1,000 words
Standard (¥100 / $14): Articles under 2,000 words, includes 1 revision
Premium (¥200 / $28): Articles under 3,000 words, includes 3 revisions, lifetime support
```

**② Built a content library**

"I organize commonly used article frameworks, industry data, and key quotes into a library. Next time I write a similar article, I can call them directly, and the efficiency is much higher."

**③ Recruited partners**

"Later, I really couldn't handle it alone, so I found 2 friends who also wanted to do a side hustle."

"I'm responsible for getting orders and quality control, and they do the writing. We split the profits."

---

### 3.3 Revenue Breakthrough

After these upgrades, Xiaolin's side hustle income exploded:

| Month | Income |
|-------|--------|
| Month 1 | ¥700 ($98) |
| Month 2 | ¥1,200 ($168) |
| Month 3 | ¥2,800 ($392) |
| Month 4 | ¥4,600 ($644) |
| Month 5 | ¥6,200 ($868) |
| Month 6 | ¥8,000 ($1,120) |

---

## 4. The Universal Lessons from Xiaolin's Story

Xiaolin's story is not unique. Thousands of ordinary people are repeating the same pattern:

1. **Don't start with "What can AI do?" Start with "What can I do?"**
2. **Pick a niche, become the best in that niche**
3. **Treat AI as a productivity tool, not a magic wand**
4. **Build a system, not just trade time for money**
5. **Quality compounds. Reputation compounds. Word of mouth compounds.**

The most valuable skill in 2026 isn't "knowing how to use AI." It's "knowing what problem to solve with AI." The bottleneck has moved from execution to taste.

---

## Final Thoughts

The AI era doesn't belong to those who can write the best prompts. It belongs to those who can identify the most valuable problems.

Xiaolin didn't know how to code. She didn't know how to build a SaaS product. She just knew that "people need year-end summaries" and "AI can write them fast."

That's enough.

**In 2026, the gap between "earning $700/month" and "earning $1,400/month on the side" isn't technical—it's the willingness to start.**
