## Enterprise Selection Decision Framework

### Enterprise Need Categories

| Enterprise Type | Typical Needs | Core Metrics |
|-----------------|---------------|--------------|
| Internet companies | Code generation, product design | Efficiency, cost |
| Traditional enterprises | Customer service, knowledge base | Stability, ease of use |
| Financial enterprises | Risk control, data analysis | Security, compliance |
| Manufacturing enterprises | Production optimization, supply chain | Stability, integration |

---

### Decision Dimensions

Enterprises need to consider 5 dimensions:

1. **Capability**: Does the model's capability meet the need
2. **Cost**: Is the procurement cost within budget
3. **Stability**: Is the service stable and reliable
4. **Security**: Is the data secure and compliant
5. **Ecosystem**: Is it easy to integrate
