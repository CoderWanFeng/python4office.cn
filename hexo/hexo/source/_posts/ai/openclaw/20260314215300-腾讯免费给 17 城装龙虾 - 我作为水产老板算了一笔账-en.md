## 🦞 The Day I Received Tencent's Call, I Thought It Was a Scam

On March 10th, I received a call from Shenzhen.

"Hello, we're from Tencent Cloud Industrial Cooperation Department, we want to talk to you about free deployment of AI lobster inspection system..."

My first reaction was: Scam?

Free? A big company like Tencent would install a system for free for a small aquatic farm like ours?

But the other party was very detailed:
- Equipment they provide
- Installation they handle
- Training they conduct
- First three months cloud service fees also waived

The only requirement: Allow them to use our factory as a case study for promotion, and cooperate to collect some desensitized data.

I hesitated for two days, and finally decided to try. Anyway, it's free. The worst case is wasting a few days.

## 📋 Installation Process: Much Simpler Than I Thought

On March 12th, Tencent's technical team came with 3 people.

I thought there would be major construction, but:

**9:00 AM** - On-site survey
- Examined our sorting assembly line
- Measured installation locations
- Confirmed power and network interfaces

**11:00 AM** - Equipment installation
- Installed 2 industrial cameras
- Deployed light system
- Connected edge computing box (just a small host)

**2:00 PM** - System debugging
- Collected sample images of 500 lobsters
- Did initial model calibration
- Tested inspection process

**5:00 PM** - Personnel training
- Taught us how to operate the backend
- Taught us how to view data dashboard
- Taught us how to handle exceptions

**Total time: 8 hours**

The next morning, the system was officially operational.

## 💰 First Week Data: I Was Amazed

Our factory is not large, daily processing volume is about 30,000 lobsters.

Before using manual inspection:
- Needed 6 workers in shifts
- 300 yuan per person per day
- Daily labor cost: 1,800 yuan
- Monthly labor cost: 54,000 yuan

First week data after system operation:

| Metric | Expected | Actual |
|--------|----------|--------|
| Inspection speed | 150 units/min | 187 units/min |
| Accuracy | 95% | 97.8% |
| Misjudgment rate | <3% | 1.4% |
| System stability | 99% | 99.7% |

**The best part**: Now only need 2 people for re-inspection and packaging, the other 4 people can do other work.

## 🎯 Second Week: Discovered New Problems

System operation was smooth, but also exposed some issues.

**Problem 1: When small and large lobsters are inspected together, accuracy decreases**

Solution: After communicating with Tencent team, they remotely updated the model, adding size pre-classification.

**Problem 2: Large light changes at night, occasional misjudgments**

Solution: Adjusted brightness auto-regulation parameters of light system.

**Problem 3: Workers have resistance to new system**

This is the funniest one. An old employee told me: "Wan Feng, will this machine take our jobs?"

I said: "No, it just helps you do the heaviest work. You do re-inspection and packaging, it's easier work."

And, because efficiency improved, we received more orders, actually needed to hire new people.

## 📊 One Month Later Cost Comparison

Now the system has been running for a full month, let me do a complete accounting.

### Before (Pure Manual)

```
Labor cost: 6 people × 300 yuan/day × 30 days = 54,000 yuan
Management cost: QC supervisor 1 person × 8,000 yuan/month = 8,000 yuan
Training cost: New employee training ~5,000 yuan/month
Loss cost: Returns due to misjudgment ~12,000 yuan/month
────────────────────────────────────
Total: 79,000 yuan/month
```

### Now (AI + Manual)

```
Labor cost: 2 people × 300 yuan/day × 30 days = 18,000 yuan
Management cost: QC supervisor 1 person × 8,000 yuan/month = 8,000 yuan
System cost: Cloud service 2,000 yuan/month (first 3 months free)
Maintenance cost: Tencent free maintenance = 0 yuan
Loss cost: Returns due to misjudgment ~3,000 yuan/month
────────────────────────────────────
Total: 31,000 yuan/month
```

**Monthly savings: 48,000 yuan**
**Annual savings: 576,000 yuan**
