## Method 1: AI Content Services (Fast Money)

**What to do:**
Help others generate content with AI—copywriting, images, video scripts, PPTs...

**My experience:**
- Wrote promotional copy orders on Xiaohongshu, 50 yuan/article
- Created product detail pages for Taobao merchants, 200 yuan/set
- Made AI training courseware for enterprises, 1000 yuan/session

**First month earnings: 4000+.**

**Pros:** Low barrier, quick results
**Cons:** Trading time for money, low ceiling

**Who it's for:** Those who want to see money fast, those with ample time

---

## Method 2: AI Tools + Automation (Scalable)

**What to do:**
Build automation systems using AI + code, then sell the system or charge service fees.

**My case:**

Built a system for a small e-commerce owner:
- Auto-monitor competitor prices
- Auto-generate price drop alerts
- Auto-adjust own pricing

**Charge:** One-time development fee 8000 + 500/month maintenance

**Key:** This system can be replicated and sold to peers.

**Pros:** Scalable, low marginal cost
**Cons:** Requires technical ability

**Who it's for:** Those who know some programming, want to build long-term business

---

## Method 3: Teaching Others to Use AI (Most Reliable)

**What to do:**
Teach others the AI skills you've learned.

**Why is it most reliable?**

Because AI is developing so fast—**there's always someone wanting to learn new things.**

**My approach:**
- Write public account articles (what you're reading now)
- Record video courses
- Run paid communities
- Accept enterprise training gigs

**Income structure:**
- Ad revenue share: 3000-5000/month
- Course sales: 10-30k/month (unstable)
- Enterprise training: 5000-20000/session (occasional)

**Total: Average monthly income 20k+.**

**Pros:** Compound interest effect, gets easier over time
**Cons:** Slow early accumulation, requires continuous output

**Who it's for:** Those willing to share, have patience to accumulate

## Pitfall Guide

A few common pitfalls to save you money:

❌ **Don't believe in "AI fully automated money-making"**
Anyone saying you can earn without lifting a finger is cutting leeks.

❌ **Don't buy expensive AI tool memberships**
Use free ones first, pay only when really needed.

❌ **Don't all-in on one platform**
Distribute across multiple platforms to reduce risk.

❌ **Don't violate platform rules**
Like using AI to batch-generate garbage content—accounts get banned.

## What Abilities Do You Need?

No matter which method you choose, these 3 abilities are important:

### 1. Prompt Engineering
Know how to communicate with AI to get good results.

### 2. Basic Programming Ability
At least know how to use Python to process data and call APIs.

### 3. Product Thinking
Know what users need, not just sell what you're good at.
