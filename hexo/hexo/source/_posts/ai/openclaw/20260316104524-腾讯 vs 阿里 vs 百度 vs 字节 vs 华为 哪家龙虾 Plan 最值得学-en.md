Hello everyone, this is programmer Wan Feng actively working on various AI projects.

All 5 major companies have launched Lobster Coding Plans. Many readers asked me: "Wan Feng, I want to learn one, which one should I choose?"

This question is very practical. After all, **learning has time costs**, choosing wrong might waste several months.

Today I spent 7 days deeply comparing 5 major companies' Lobster Plans from 8 dimensions, giving you an objective purchasing guide.

| Company | Overall Score | Recommended Users | Learning Cost |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|---------------|
| Tencent | 4.6/5 | General developers, OpenClaw users | Low |
| Alibaba | 4.1/5 | Alibaba Cloud ecosystem enterprises | Medium |
| ByteDance | 3.8/5 | E-commerce, content creators | Low |
| Huawei | 3.7/5 | Hardware needs, government projects | High |
| Baidu | 3.3/5 | Offline training needs | Medium |
