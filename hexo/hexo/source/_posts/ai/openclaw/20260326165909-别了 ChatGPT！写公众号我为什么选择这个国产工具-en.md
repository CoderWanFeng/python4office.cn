## 🤖 A Counterintuitive View

In today's world full of AI tools:

- Someone writes articles with ChatGPT
- Someone polishes with Claude
- Someone formats with Notion AI

**But I, chose a "simple" domestic formatting tool.**

White Water AI Community's Markdown formatting tool:
https://python4office.cn/md2mp/

**No AI, no intelligence, just pure formatting.**

Why?

## 🎯 Reason 1: AI is Not All-Powerful

### What can AI do?

- ✅ Generate article drafts
- ✅ Polish text
- ✅ Check typos
- ✅ Provide writing suggestions

### What can't AI do?

- ❌ Replace your thinking
- ❌ Provide real experience
- ❌ Build personal style
- ❌ **Format WeChat official accounts**

**Yes, ChatGPT can't format WeChat articles.**

You ask ChatGPT to format:

```
Please help me format this article into WeChat official account format...
```

It gives you a bunch of Markdown, then what?

**You still have to manually copy to WeChat backend, manually adjust.**

**AI can't solve the last mile.**

## 🛠️ Reason 2: Leave Professional Work to Professional Tools

### AI Writing Tools

- Advantages: Content generation
- Disadvantages: Format formatting

### WeChat Article Formatting Tools

- Advantages: Format formatting
- Disadvantages: Content generation

**Division of labor is the right way.**

My workflow:

```
1. Think with your brain (AI can't replace)
2. Write in Markdown (simple and efficient)
3. Format with formatting tool (professional match)
4. Copy to WeChat and publish (perfect connection)
```

**No AI, but higher efficiency.**

## 🇨🇳 Reason 3: Domestic Tools Better Understand Chinese Scenarios

### Pain Points of Foreign Tools

- Insufficient Chinese formatting optimization

- Servers abroad, slow access
- Paid, USD pricing

### Advantages of Domestic Tools

- Optimized for Chinese
- Perfect adaptation to WeChat
- Domestic servers, instant open
- Free, no payment traps

**White Water AI Community's this tool**:

- Font: Optimized for Chinese reading (15px most comfortable)
- Line spacing: 1.75x (best for Chinese reading)
- Code: Supports Chinese comments
- Copy: One-click paste to WeChat, format stays intact

**These details, foreign tools won't do.**
