## 🦞 Why Lobster?

Before starting, I was also confused: Why lobster? Not cat/dog recognition, not face recognition,，偏偏是 lobster?

After in-depth research, I found **3 deep reasons** behind this:

### 1. Large Enough Market
- 2025 China lobster industry total output: 450 billion yuan
- Practitioners: Over 10 million
- AI penetration: Less than 5%, huge growth space

### 2. Typical Technical Scenarios
Lobster recognition covers multiple core AI scenarios:
- Image recognition (freshness detection)
- Classification algorithms (variety, specifications)
- Time series prediction (price fluctuations)
- Decision optimization (procurement strategy)

### 3. Adequate Policy Support
- Ministry of Agriculture "Digital Village" pilot project
- Local government subsidies for AI agricultural applications
- Rural revitalization special fund support

## 📊 5 Major Companies Horizontal Comparison

### Tencent - Lobster AI Developer Plan

**Advantages**:
- ✅ Deep integration with OpenClaw, smooth development experience
- ✅ Provides real lobster farm data (100,000+ images)
- ✅ WeChat Work integration, convenient for team collaboration
- ✅ Free Tencent Cloud resources (worth 5000 yuan)

**Disadvantages**:
- ⚠️ Documentation updates slightly slow, some APIs changed but not synced
- ⚠️ Community Q&A response time ~6 hours

**Practical Results**:
```python
from openclaw import skill
from tencent.loops import LobsterDetector

@skill.register
def detect_lobster(image_path: str) -> dict:
    """Lobster freshness detection"""
    detector = LobsterDetector(model="freshness-v2")
    result = detector.predict(image_path)
    return {
        "fresh": result.confidence > 0.85,
        "grade": result.grade,  # A/B/C/D
        "price_suggestion": result.suggested_price
    }
```

**Rating**: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (4.5/5)
