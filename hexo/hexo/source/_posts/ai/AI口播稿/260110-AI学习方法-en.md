## **Script: The "Three-Step Funnel" Method for Ordinary People to Systematically Learn AI (Avoid 99% of Pitfalls)**

**(Video style suggestion: narration + key step graphics. Steady and powerful speaking pace, conveying a reliable mentor feel)**

---

**【0:00-0:20 Opening - Create Resonance】**

(Facing the camera, sincere expression, slightly "revealing secrets" feeling)

"Are you wanting to learn AI but got scared off by various models and algorithms right away? Or maybe you've bookmarked a bunch of tools, but haven't really understood any of them?"

(Pause briefly, tone becomes firm)

"Today, I'm giving you an absolutely different answer. I've summarized a **'Three-Step Funnel' learning method** using nearly 10 years of technical experience. It won't make you an expert overnight, but it will guarantee you **avoid 99% of pitfalls** and take the shortest path from 'anxious beginner' to 'AI money-making practitioner.'"

**【0:20-1:10 Step One: Perception Layer (Week 1) - Build a Panorama Map】**

(Background can switch to "Step One: Perception Layer | Build Map" graphics)

"**The first step is not to learn, but to observe.** I call this the 'Perception Layer.' Give yourself one week, absolutely no complex theories."

"Your core tasks are just two:
**First, use them.** Treat the hottest products like ChatGPT, Wenxin Yiyan, Midjourney as toys—play with them all. Don't worry about principles, just ask them weird questions, have them write poems, make spreadsheets, create stories.
**Second, browse.** Go to my 'Plain Water AI' community, Bilibili, Xiaohongshu, and see those highly-rated AI videos—what specific problems are others solving with AI?"

"The goal of this step is to **build an 'AI capability world map' in your brain.** You know approximately where AI's boundaries are, what ready-made weapons you can use. Many people's mistake is jumping directly into a valley to mine without even having a map. How could they not get lost?"

**【1:10-2:10 Step Two: Application Layer (Month 1) - Choose One Sword】**

(Background switches to "Step Two: Application Layer | Focus on Practice" graphics)

"With the map, the second step is most critical: **Application Layer.** Based on your profession and pain points, **select the smallest area on the map and break through it.**"

"For example, if you're in design, obsess over AI painting; if you're a copywriter, focus on large language model prompts; if you're a programmer like me or want to do side hustles, then **Python automation + AI** is your trump card."

(Emphasize here, can add gestures)
"Why do I especially recommend 'Python + AI'? Because it gives you **the ability to create tools**, not just use them. You can turn AI into an automated employee. My 'Python Office Automation' course is designed to build this foundation for you."

"This month, your goal isn't to 'know,' it's to 'do.' Use just this one direction you've chosen to solve a real work problem, even if it's just using AI to automatically organize a weekly report. **Completion is a hundred times more important than perfection.**"

**【2:10-2:50 Step Three: Principle Layer (Continuous) - Master Car Repair Skills】**

(Background switches to "Step Three: Principle Layer | Continuous Improvement" graphics)

"When you get comfortable using it, you'll naturally hit bottlenecks: Why does AI sometimes talk nonsense? How can I make it more obedient? Only then does it naturally lead to **Step Three: Principle Layer.**"

"At this point, when you see words like 'Transformer' or 'fine-tuning,' they won't be hieroglyphics anymore. Because you're carrying specific questions, you know why you need to learn it—it's like when your car breaks down, you're more motivated to learn car repair."

"This step has no end, but it becomes an 'on-demand learning' game. You'll climb up the knowledge tree yourself, understanding more as you go. And this is exactly the journey my new course with Posts & Telecom Press will systematically take you through: **from application to principle, building a complete AI knowledge framework for you.**"

**【2:50-End Summary and Call to Action】**

(Camera cuts back to close-up of person, clean background)

"So remember this 'Three-Step Funnel': **First broadly observe to build perception, then focus on deep application, finally investigate principles on-demand.** This order must never be wrong."

"If you don't want to figure it out yourself and want to take the smoothest path:
**First, follow me.** I will, in the **live streams** at the 'Plain Water AI' community, break down each step of this 'Three-Step Funnel' into specific resource lists and operation manuals;
**Second, if you agree 'Python + AI' is a good path,** check out my existing Python course—it's the most solid springboard for you to enter the second layer;
**Third, stay tuned.** This year my brand new AI course is a systematic guide that takes you through this entire path."

(Looking sincerely at the camera)
"I'm Programmer Wanfeng. I don't talk about mysticism, only paths that ordinary people can walk. See you next time."
