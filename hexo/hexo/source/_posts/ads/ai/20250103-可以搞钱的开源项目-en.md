Here are some open-source projects that can help you make money, along with brief introductions:

1. **list-of-wechat-mini-program-list**
   - An open-source WeChat mini-program list containing a large number of mini-programs that can help you make money.
   - Honor of Kings Story Site: Mini-program with open-sourced full data scraping code, content site related to gaming.
   - Avatar with Mask Mini-Program: Quickly implemented using uniapp with Vue, accumulated 100k+ users, generated 4k+ in ad revenue.
   - Zhihu Daily: Using mini-programs for content, finding the right topics, and optimizing for mini-program SEO.
   - Rebate Coupon Mini-Program Template: E-commerce coupon direction, close to money, can generate sustainable income.
   - Meituan/Eleme Red Packet: Food delivery red packets, CPS commission, others claim red packets and place orders, you earn commissions.
   - WeChat Red Packet Cover Collection: Users watch video ads or invite users to get WeChat red packet serial numbers.
   - Account Assistant WeChat Mini-Program: A mini-program for storing accounts and passwords, using WeChat mini-program cloud development, no server needed.
   - Old Huangli WeChat Mini-Program: Supports checking auspicious/inauspicious events, fortune-telling is a huge industry with large profits.
   - Succulent Talk: Succulent plant encyclopedia mini-program, succulents are addictive, huge industry potential.
   - [Project Address](https://github.com/shaoxiongdu/list-of-wechat-mini-program-list)

2. **mfish-nocode**
   - Mofish Low-Code Platform aims to build a no-code platform based on low-code, usable by programmers and also meeting non-professional needs.
   - Tech Stack: Backend based on Spring Cloud Alibaba ecosystem, frontend developed based on Vue-Vben-Admin.
   - [Project Address](https://gitee.com/qiufeng9862/mfish-nocode)

3. **Other Open-Source Projects**
   - iparking: Parking fee management system.
   - ThinkAdmin: Fast development backend management system following MIT protocol.
   - Hotel-System: Smart hotel management system.
   - Himool ERP: Open-source ERP management system.
   - Pig Admin: Enterprise-level fast development backend management system.
   - oa_system: OA office automation system.
   - FJ-OMS SKYER: E-commerce middle platform OMS omni-channel order management system.
   - gfast: Go Frame (GF) backend management system.
   - Fantastic-admin: An out-of-the-box Vue middle/backend management system framework.
   - shangm<br/>A: Free grade management system from CodeAnt.
   - rebuild_CRM_ERP: Inventory production management system.
   - WML: Asset library management system.
   - react-admin: Minimalist backend management system.
   - nodcloud: Dianke Cloud inventory management system.
   - [Project List](https://blog.csdn.net/Ed7zgeE9X/article/details/131629619)

These open-source projects cover multiple fields from mini-program development to enterprise management systems, providing developers with broad commercial opportunities.
