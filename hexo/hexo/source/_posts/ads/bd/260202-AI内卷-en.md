OpenClaw is completely crazy! The fastest viral AI in history, even Mac mini is sold out everywhere! 🚀

Friends who follow AI recently should have seen that an open source AI project called OpenClaw (originally called Clawdbot, then Moltbot) is skyrocketing like a rocket! 🔥

Putting aside technical indicators, let's talk about one data point everyone can understand: because of its good compatibility, Mac mini has become the preferred device to run it, directly causing Mac mini to be sold out on global e-commerce platforms. Yes, an AI software actually caused computer hardware to sell out! Can you believe that? 🤯

Why is it so popular? Because it's not an ordinary AI at all! It can completely take over your computer, acting like a 7*24 online assistant to help you complete work.

This kind of down-to-earth product is the AI innovation we ordinary people want.

But what are we doing?

Recently, when you open your phone, are you bombarded by major internet companies' "Spring Festival marketing" again? Naming evening parties, red envelope rains, advertising wars... year after year, competing in circles, it's just the old routine of spending money to circle traffic and grab users.

Marketing costs of hundreds of millions or even billions of yuan sound scary. If even a small part of this money and energy was invested in product innovation and technology research like OpenClaw, wouldn't the next disruptive product have been born long ago?

When others are exploring how to use AI to reshape productivity, can we only stay in the involution game of "subsidy for new users"?

Traffic will dry up, subsidies will stop, but the value brought by real innovation is eternal.

In 2026, stop staring only at immediate traffic data. Maybe it's time to look further ahead, to create, not just to compete in an involutionary way.
