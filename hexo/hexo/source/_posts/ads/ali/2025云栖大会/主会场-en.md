The morning Yunqi Conference was so exciting!

Sharing a few viewpoints that impressed me:

The first point comes from Mama Wu (Wu Yongming): AGI is just the starting point, and Super Artificial Intelligence (ASI) is the ultimate goal.

Based on this target route, Alibaba will implement its AI strategy through two core paths: First, Tongyi Qianwen firmly follows the open source and openness route, committed to creating the "Android of the AI era"; second, building a super AI cloud as the "next-generation computer" to provide an intelligent computing power network for the world.

The second point comes from the release of new Tongyi products: The brand-new Tongyi Wanxiang 2.5 has achieved a comprehensive upgrade in creative capabilities. Inputting an image and a paragraph of text, Tongyi Wanxiang can generate synchronized audio-visual videos. Starting from July this year, my content creation has also fully shifted to AI, so I pay special attention to video generation large models. Everyone should try it out too!
