This year's Cloud Computing Conference theme is "Integration of Cloud and Intelligence, Coexistence of Carbon and Silicon". I visited three major theme exhibition halls: Artificial Intelligence + Hall, Computing Hall, and Cutting-edge Application Hall.  

💻 In the Artificial Intelligence + Hall, the Tongyi large model family is arranged as a "capability wall". The code large model writes Python on site, and its downloads have soared to 600 million. I casually entered a function requirement, and it returned a complete runnable script in seconds. It's really easy to use.

🙋 The Computing Hall turns "cloud" into an amusement park: Wuying cloud computers are installed in racing cockpits, and with a step on the accelerator, 3A games run at full 4K resolution; next to it, robots dance to DJ music, and children are super excited, full of technological sense.  

🤖 The Cutting-edge Application Hall is more like a future market: quadruped robots that can do somersaults, robotic arms that can sell snacks by scanning codes, self-driving cars, and rows of Agent booths. You can even ask AI to write PPT for you while ordering a cup of coffee.  

🎁 Don't forget to bring a big backpack after visiting the three halls! Alibaba Cloud stickers, Wuying limited umbrellas, robot keychains, and green power blind boxes are available in abundance. I saw friends online got a whole bag of them.
