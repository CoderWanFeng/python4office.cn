## Huawei Really Knows How to Name Things

Let's not talk about the pros and cons of this programming language first. Let's praise the name of this language first.

When naming technology products, Huawei consciously integrates elements of traditional Chinese culture, especially drawing inspiration from ancient Chinese mythology and legends.

Here are some summaries of Huawei technology product names in classical Chinese:

1. **Kirin**: Huawei's smartphone chip series, named "Kirin." Kirin was regarded as an auspicious mythical beast in ancient China, symbolizing auspiciousness and power.

2. **Kunpeng**: Huawei's PC processor chip, named "Kunpeng." Kunpeng is a giant fish and bird from ancient Chinese mythology, symbolizing transformation and unlimited potential.

3. **HarmonyOS**: Huawei's operating system, named "Hongmeng." Hongmeng represents the chaotic state when the universe first began in ancient Chinese mythology, symbolizing creation and unlimited possibilities.

4. **Kunlun**, **Penglai**, **Xuanyuan**, **Taishan**: Huawei's cloud server naming, taken from mountain names in "Classic of Mountains and Seas," implying lofty aspirations and the spirit of exploration.

5. **Dangkang**, **Qingniao**, **Taotie**, **Huangma**: Huawei's chip naming, taken from mythical beasts in "Classic of Mountains and Seas," implying auspiciousness and unlimited imagination for future technology.

6. **Zhurong**, **Chongli**, **Dayu**, **Jingwei**: Huawei's patent naming, taken from characters or deity names in "Classic of Mountains and Seas," implying courage, determination, and innovative spirit.

These names not only reflect Huawei's respect for and inheritance of traditional culture, but also showcase its product philosophy and corporate culture, combining ancient mythology with modern technology to create unique brand identifiers.
