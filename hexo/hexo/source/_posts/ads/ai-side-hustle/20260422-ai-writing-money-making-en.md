## 🎯 Step 1: Positioning

First, clarify what content you want to write.

**Direction suggestions:**

| Direction | Features | Audience |
|---------|----------|----------|
| Technical Tutorials | Precise audience, strong willingness to pay | Programmers, students |
| Workplace Tips | Large audience, strong demand | Workplace people |
| Emotional Content | Fast spreading, easy to gain followers | Young people |
| Parent-Child Education | Strong willingness to pay | Parents |

**How to choose:**

1. Look at what you're good at
2. Look at what the market needs
3. Look at what you like

Now start writing content.

1. Valuable, useful to readers
2. Has style, different from others
3. Has rhythm, regular updates
