## 💰 Direction 1: AI Writing Services

**Difficulty: ⭐⭐**

**Income: Monthly 3k-10k**

**Suitable for: Those who can write or are willing to learn**

**What to do:**

- Help others write articles, copywriting, and emails
- Help students with homework and papers (Note: Only tutoring, not ghostwriting)
- Help businesses write promotional copy and press releases

**How to do it:**

1. First use AI to generate a draft
2. Manually polish and modify
3. Deliver to the client

**How to find clients:**

- Xiaohongshu, Zhihu, Bilibili - share AI writing skills to attract traffic
- Taobao, Pinduoduo - open shops
- WeChat Moments, communities - take orders
