---

## ❌ Pitfall 1: Don't go all in at the beginning

Many people go all in right away, quitting their jobs to do AI side hustles full-time.

**Why it's a trap:**
- AI side hustles need time to build up, not immediate income
- You might not make money at first, creating a lot of pressure
- If you fail, you have no fallback

**Correct approach:**
- Start part-time, run through the minimum viable loop
- Only consider full-time when you have stable income

---

## ❌ Pitfall 2: Don't speculate, build accumulation

Many people want quick money, doing this today and that tomorrow.

**Why it's a trap:**
- No accumulation, nothing done deeply
- Can't earn sustainable income
- Wastes time, accomplishes nothing

**Correct approach:**
- Choose one direction, keep deepening
- Accumulate fans, accumulate works
- Build personal brand

---

## ❌ Pitfall 3: Don't violate rules, stay compliant

Many people use AI to do illegal things like ghostwriting papers, content spinning, plagiarism.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Illegal things don't last
- May get banned, even legal issues
- Too risky, not worth it

**Correct approach:**
- Do compliant things
- Original content or with authorization
- Follow platform rules

---

## ❌ Pitfall 4: Don't think about getting rich overnight

Many people want to get rich overnight, expecting monthly income of over 10k after a few days.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Unrealistic, easy to get disappointed
- Easy to be scammed, be taken advantage of
- Mentality easily collapses, can't persist

**Correct approach:**
- Lower expectations, take it slow
- Small steps, continuous accumulation
- Take a long-term view, focus on long-term returns

---

## ❌ Pitfall 5: Don't just learn without doing

Many people keep learning, taking many courses, learning many tools, but never start doing.

**Why it's a trap:**
- No matter how much you learn, not doing equals zero
- No feedback, don't know how well you're learning
- Wastes time and money

**Correct approach:**
- Learn while doing, learn through practice
- Small cost testing, run through minimum viable loop
- Adjust based on feedback

---

## ❌ Pitfall 6: Don't work alone

Many people work alone, not communicating, not joining communities.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Information闭塞, don't know latest trends
- Easy to take wrong paths
- No resources, no partners

**Correct approach:**
- Join relevant communities
- Communicate with peers
- Learn from each other, help each other

---

## ❌ Pitfall 7: Don't neglect personal brand

Many people only think about taking orders to make money, not valuing personal brand.

**Why it's a trap:**
- No personal brand, unstable order flow
- Personal brand can bring passive income
- Personal brand is a long-term asset

**Correct approach:**
- Value personal brand building
- Share content on various platforms
- Build trust, accumulate fans

---

## ❌ Pitfall 8: Don't neglect intellectual property

Many people use AI to generate content without paying attention to intellectual property.

**Why it's a trap:**
- May have copyright issues
- May be complained about
- Not worth it

**Correct approach:**
- Use authorized tools
- Try to be original
- Understand intellectual property knowledge

---

## ❌ Pitfall 9: Don't neglect data security

Many people use various AI tools without paying attention to data security.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Sensitive data may leak
- May be used for training
- Not worth it

**Correct approach:**
- Remember to anonymize sensitive data
- Use reputable tools
- Understand data security knowledge

---

## ❌ Pitfall 10: Don't neglect health

Many people do side hustles, staying up late, not exercising, ruining their health.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Health is the foundation of revolution
- If health collapses, nothing matters
- Not worth it

**Correct approach:**
- Pay attention to rest, don't stay up late
- Exercise regularly, keep healthy
- Take a long-term view, steady progress
