Brothers!!!

Want to do an AI side hustle but afraid of falling into traps?

**I've seen too many people fall into this.**

I'm Programmer Wan Feng, 300k+ followers across platforms, author of the python-office open source project.

Today I'm telling you about 10 common traps.

---

## ❌ Trap 1: Don't Go All In at the Start

Many people go all in from the start, quit their job and do AI side hustle full-time.

**Why it's a trap:**
- AI side hustle needs time to accumulate, not immediately profitable
- May not earn money at first, huge pressure
- If you fail, no backup plan

**Correct approach:**
- Do it part-time first, run through the minimum loop
- Only consider full-time when you have stable income

---

## ❌ Trap 2: Don't Speculate, Accumulate

Many people want to make quick money, do this today, that tomorrow.

**Why it's a trap:**
- No accumulation, can't do anything deeply
- Can't earn continuous income
- Time wasted, nothing accomplished

**Correct approach:**
- Choose one direction, keep digging deep
- Accumulate followers, accumulate works
- Build personal brand

---

## ❌ Trap 3: Don't Violate Rules, Be Compliant

Many people use AI to do some illegal things, like ghostwriting papers, plagiarizing, copying.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Illegal things can't last long
- May get banned, even legal issues
- Risk too high, not worth it

**Correct approach:**
- Do compliant things
- Original content, or with authorization
- Follow platform rules

---

## ❌ Trap 4: Don't Think About Getting Rich Overnight

Many people want to get rich overnight, do it for a few days and want to earn over 10k/month.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Not realistic, easy to be disappointed
- Easy to be scammed, get cut as a "leek"
- Mindset easily collapses, can't persist

**Correct approach:**
- Lower expectations, take it slow
- Small steps and fast iterations, keep accumulating
- Extend the time horizon, look at long-term gains

---

## ❌ Trap 5: Don't Just Study Without Doing

Many people keep studying, study many courses, many tools, but never start doing.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Studying more without doing equals zero
- No feedback, don't know how well you're learning
- Waste time and money

**Correct approach:**
- Learn while doing, learn in practice
- Small cost testing, run through minimum loop
- Adjust based on feedback

---

## ❌ Trap 6: Don't Go It Alone

Many people do things with their head down alone, don't communicate, don't join communities.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Information closed, don't know latest developments
- Easy to take detours
- No resources, no partners

**Correct approach:**
- Join relevant communities
- Communicate with peers
- Learn from each other, help each other

---

## ❌ Trap 7: Don't Neglect Personal Brand

Many people only think about taking orders to make money, don't emphasize personal brand.

**Why it's a trap:**
- Without personal brand, orders are unstable
- Personal brand can bring passive income
- Personal brand is a long-term asset

**Correct approach:**
- Emphasize personal brand building
- Share content on various platforms
- Build trust, accumulate followers

---

## ❌ Trap 8: Don't Neglect Intellectual Property

Many people use AI to generate content without paying attention to intellectual property.

**Why it's a trap:**
- May have copyright issues
- May get complaints
- Gains don't offset losses

**Correct approach:**
- Use tools with authorization
- Try to be original
- Understand intellectual property related knowledge
