## 🚀 Step 1: Register an Account

Super simple, 3 minutes to complete:

**Steps:**
1. Open AtomGit official website
2. Click register, use phone number or email
3. Complete registration, log in

---

## 👤 Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Let more people know you:

**Things to do:**
1. Upload avatar (your own photo is better)
2. Write a bio (what you do, what you know)
3. Add skill tags (Python, AI programming, etc.)

---

## 👥 Step 3: Find Interesting Content

Start exploring the community:

**Things you can do:**
1. Search for projects you're interested in
2. Like and comment
3. Follow people you like
4. Join discussions

---

## 💪 Step 4: Start Contributing

From spectator to participant:

**How to contribute:**
1. Submit Issues (report bugs or suggest features)
2. Submit PRs (modify code)
3. Share your projects
4. Write technical articles
