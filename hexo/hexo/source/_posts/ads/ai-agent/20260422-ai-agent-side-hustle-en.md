## 💰 Case 1: AI Writing, Monthly Income 5k

**Background:**

Xiao Wang is an office worker who likes writing and wants to do a side hustle.

**How he did it:**

1. Used AI Agent to help others write articles and copywriting
2. AI generates the first draft, Xiao Wang manually polishes it
3. Takes orders on Xiaohongshu and Zhihu

**Results:**

First month: **3k**, second month: **5k**.
