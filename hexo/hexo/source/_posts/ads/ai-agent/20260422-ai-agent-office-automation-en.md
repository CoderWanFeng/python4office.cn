## 📧 Case 1: Automatic Email Processing

**Requirement:**

100 emails every day that need to be categorized, replied to, and forwarded.

**How AI Agent does it:**

1. Automatic categorization: important, general, spam
2. Important emails notify you, general emails get automatic replies
3. Spam emails get deleted directly
4. Emails that need forwarding get forwarded automatically

**Results:**

What used to take 1 hour now gets done in **5 minutes**.
