## 1. Why Choose Coding Plan?

First, why choose the subscription model like Coding Plan:

### Traditional vs Coding Plan

| Comparison | Traditional (Pay as you go) | Coding Plan (Subscription) |
|-----------|---------------------------|--------------------------|
| Cost | Pay for what you use | Fixed monthly fee |
| Token anxiety | Every use decreases | Unlimited within subscription |
| Budget control | Easy to overspend | Fixed budget |
| Suitable for | Large companies, precise cost control | Personal developers, small teams |

**Core advantage of Coding Plan: Save worry, no anxiety, high value for money.**
