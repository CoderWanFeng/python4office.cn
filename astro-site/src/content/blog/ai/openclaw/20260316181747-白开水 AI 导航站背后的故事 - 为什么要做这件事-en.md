Hello everyone, this is programmer Wan Feng actively working on various AI projects.

White Water AI navigation site is released (https://www.python4office.cn/ai-nav/).

But you might not know, this navigation site took整整 2 months from idea to launch.

Today, I want to talk about **the story behind the navigation site**.

## 🎬 Origin: A Question in a Group

In January 2026, I saw a message in an AI learning group:

> "各位大佬，想找个 AI 写 PPT 的工具，搜了半天全是广告，有没有靠谱推荐的？"

This question sparked heated discussion:
- Someone recommended Gamma
- Someone recommended Beautiful.ai
- Someone said use ChatGPT to generate content

But the question is: **Why is it so hard to find a tool?**

## 🤔 Research: Discovered Three Pain Points

In the following 1 week, I did a small research in the community, collecting 200+ feedbacks.

### Pain Point 1: Information Overload

**Data**: 78% of users said "too many AI tools, don't know which to choose"

**Reasons**:
- 10+ tools launching daily
- Information scattered across platforms
- No systematic organization

### Pain Point 2: Choice Difficulty

**Data**: 65% of users "spend lots of time comparing tools, still hesitant in the end"

**Reasons**:
- Similar functions for same-type tools
- Non-transparent pricing
- Lack of objective comparison

### Pain Point 3: Information Lag

**Data**: 82% of users "discover good tools when they're already outdated"

**Reasons**:
- Tools update and iterate quickly
- Lack of continuous tracking
- Slow information transmission

## 💡 Idea: Build a Navigation Site

In mid-January 2026, I proposed the "AI navigation site" idea.

**Core approach**:
1. Collect mainstream AI tools
2. Categorize and organize
3. Provide objective information
4. Continuous update and maintenance

## 🚧 Development: 2-Month Journey

### Month 1: Content Collection

**Work**:
- Collect mainstream AI tools
- Organize categorization system
- Write tool introductions

**Results**: 150+ initial tools

**Difficulties**:
- Incomplete tool information
- Frequently changing prices
- Need to verify one by one

### Month 2: Website Development

**Work**:
- Design website structure
- Develop frontend pages
- Implement search functionality

**Results**: Navigation site MVP

**Difficulties**:
- Responsive design
- Loading speed optimization
- User experience polishing

## 🔑 Key Decisions

### Decision 1: Completely Free

**Discussion**: Charge or accept ads?

**Conclusion**: Completely free

**Reasons**:
- Maintain objectivity
- Lower usage barrier
- Serve community users

### Decision 2: Only Official Links

**Discussion**: Include third-party links?

**Conclusion**: Only official websites

**Reasons**:
- Avoid clone websites
