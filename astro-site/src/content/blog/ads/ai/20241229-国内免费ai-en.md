The following are GPT software that can be used for free in China:

1. **ERNIE Bot**: An AI dialogue model launched by Baidu, supporting both Chinese and English input/output, with powerful text generation, knowledge Q&A, and other functions.
2. **Tongyi Qianwen**: An AI dialogue model launched by Alibaba Cloud, also supporting both Chinese and English input/output, with text generation, knowledge Q&A, and other functions.
3. **iFlytek Spark**: An AI dialogue model launched by iFlytek, supporting both Chinese and English input/output, with text generation, knowledge Q&A, and other functions.
4. **ChatGLM**: An AI dialogue model launched by Zhipu AI, supporting both Chinese and English input/output, with text generation, knowledge Q&A, and other functions.
5. **Xiaoduo AI**: An AI dialogue model launched by Xiaoduo Technology, supporting both Chinese and English input/output, with text generation, knowledge Q&A, and other functions.
6. **Yiyan AI**: An AI dialogue model launched by NetEase, supporting both Chinese and English input/output, with text generation, knowledge Q&A, and other functions.
7. **Tencent Hunyuan**: An AI dialogue model launched by Tencent, supporting both Chinese and English input/output, with text generation, knowledge Q&A, and other functions.
