## 1. Who Is It For?

| Audience | Reason |
|------|------|

| **Small-to-Medium Teams** | Acceptable cost-performance, stable service |

Zhipu AI Coding Plan's positioning: **Developer-Friendly Domestic LLM Service**.

Strengths:
